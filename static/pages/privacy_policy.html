D. Web Beacons

A “Web Beacon” is an object that is embedded in a web page or email and is usually invisible to the user. It allows website operators to determine whether a user has viewed a particular page or email. The Company may use Web Beacons on the Platform and in emails to count users who have visited specific pages, viewed emails, or to deliver co-branded services.

Web Beacons are not used to access users’ personal information. They are used to compile aggregated statistics about Platform usage and collect only limited information — such as a Web Cookie number, the time and date of a page or email view, and a brief description of the viewed content.

You cannot directly disable Web Beacons, but they can be rendered ineffective by rejecting all cookies or setting your browser to prompt before accepting cookies.
